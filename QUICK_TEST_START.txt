## Full Workflow

1. **Login** → Use credentials above
2. **Find Products** → Use search box
3. **Add Items** → Click products or variants
4. **Fill Details** → Customer name, phone, payment method
5. **Generate Bill** → Click button
6. **View History** → Click menu/link to see past bills
7. **Print** → Click print icon

---

## What's Stored

- Bills: Browser's localStorage
- Disappears: When you clear browser data
- Persists: Until cache cleared
